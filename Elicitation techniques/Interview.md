ELICITATION TECHNIQUE: INTERVIEW

Overview: We are gathering requirements and expectations from our interviewees/stakeholders 

Stakeholders to interview :
Law students
Person working in a similar industry ( has worked in training law-related LLM, which translates legal documents in different languages)
Common People/Users 


General Interview Scheme : 
Mode of interview: Voice/Video call, one-to-one interview
Average duration of interview: 20-30 minutes
Documentation: Notes 

Interview Questionnaire: 

Law Students (GNLU, Nirma, GLS): 

What challenges do you face while handling any kind of legal documents?
Do you feel the need for any kind of assistance for the same?
How frequently do you find yourself confused in understanding legal documents? 
Would you rely on any kind of AI-powered tool for analyzing such documents?
What concerns do you have if legal documents were to be generated by LLMs (eg:- security, reliability)?
If an option to connect with a legal expert/lawyer is also provided for personalized help in addition to AI features, will it be helpful?

Person working in a similar industry (Employee working in Sarvam AI, company working on LLM that translates legal documents into local languages) : 

How do you handle the hallucination of the model?
What are the main difficulties you face while training the model?
Which type of documents do you feed to the model?
Which format have you specified for the generation of the documents?
How do you maintain the privacy of the clients?
Where do you see AI being used in this law-related industry in the near future?
What is the token limit of the model?
What type of stamp/signature do you use to authenticate the documents?
Where do you gather relevant information from?

Users/Clients : 

How do you resolve the confusion regarding any kind of legal queries currently?
How often do you face difficulty in understanding legal documents? 
If you could use a simple app or website to generate a legal document, what features would you expect it to have?
Would you find it useful if a system explained legal doubts/queries in easy/layman's language?
How important is it for you that a real lawyer verifies the document prepared by AI?
What fears or hesitations do you have in using a system that generates documents/summaries using AI?

Summary of Interview :
 
Law Students: Law students often struggle with complex language and formatting in legal documents, leading to confusion. They seek assistance, especially simplified explanations and summaries. Many are open to using AI tools but remain concerned about security and reliability. Having the option to consult a lawyer alongside AI is considered very helpful.
	
Common Users: Users often rely on friends, online searches, or local lawyers to resolve legal queries, but still face frequent difficulty understanding documents. They want a simple app that generates documents, explains terms in layman’s language, and offers lawyer verification. Concerns mainly include accuracy, data privacy, and over-reliance on AI.
	
Employee: They gather information from government websites like high court ahmedabad, many formatting issues faced by them are resolved manually , and which will be improved in the future.
	
Functional Requirements : 

Document Generation and Formatting: The system shall allow users to generate legal documents through prompts.
Document Review and Analysis: The system shall allow users to upload the documents and then give a suitable summary/analysis of the document.
Connecting to Lawyers: The system provides a platform for clients to connect to lawyers when facing any kind of difficulty.
E-signature and Authentication: The system shall support digital/e-signature and verify the documents.
Revenue Opportunities: The system shall provide a premium feature to connect to the Lawyers.
User Account & Document Management: The system shall enable users to create accounts and manage their legal documents, which are stored for further use.

Non-Functional Requirements : 

Security/Privacy: End-to-end encryption of the data provided by the user for added privacy.
Scalability: Handle multiple users simultaneously without crashing.
Performance: The system should respond to user interactions quickly without any delay.
Transparency: Provide the difference between AI-generated documents and lawyer-verified documents, and give % accuracy.
Accuracy: Minimise hallucinations and maintain a threshold percentage of accuracy.
Usability: Provide an easy-to-use interface for both lawyers and users.
Reliability: Only uses trusted LLMs, and lawyers available on the system are verified personnel.

User Stories : 


Document Summary: 	

Front card: As a user, I want the system to explain legal documents in easy language so that I can understand them easily.

Back card: System highlights difficult clauses and provides simple explanations. The system should not mislead users in case of something that the system is unsure about, and suggest that this section might need lawyers’ assistance.

Document Generation: 

Front Card: As a user, I want to generate legal documents by                providing the necessary inputs so that I can use the generated documents wherever I want to use them.

Back Card: The System should not take a very long time to generate the required documents. The generated documents should be properly formatted as per the jurisdictional parameters.

Document Verification and Authentication :
 
Front Card: As a user, I want my generated documents to be verified and signed by experts so that I can trust their legal validity.
Back Card: The system should notify users once verification is completed. The system should provide a proper stamp to barcode/stamp to authenticate the document.

Communication with Lawyers/Legal Experts : 

Front Card: As a user, I want to communicate with lawyers/legal experts through the system so that I can get personalized help for my legal needs.

Back Card: The system should provide chat features to communicate with the lawyer. And for further communication contact information of the lawyer will also be provided after the lawyer’s consent. 

Security and Privacy : 

Front Card: As a user, I want my documents and personal information to remain private and secure so that I can safely use the system.

Back Card : All user data and documents must be encrypted end-to-end. Access should require secure authentication.
