- **Overview**  
  We are gathering requirements and expectations from our interviewees/stakeholders.  

- **Stakeholders to Interview**  
  - Law Students  
  - Industry Professionals (AI in Legal Domain)  
  - Common Users  

---

- **General Interview Scheme**  
  - Mode of Interview: Voice/Video call, one-to-one interview  
  - Average Duration: 20–30 minutes  
  - Documentation: Notes  

---

- **Interview Questionnaire**

- *Law Students (GNLU, Nirma, GLS)*  
1. What challenges do you face while handling any kind of legal documents?  
2. Do you feel the need for any kind of assistance for the same?  
3. How frequently do you find yourself confused in understanding legal documents?  
4. Would you rely on any kind of AI-powered tool for analyzing such documents?  
5. What concerns do you have if legal documents were to be generated by LLMs (e.g., security, reliability)?  
6. If an option to connect with a legal expert/lawyer is also provided for personalized help in addition to AI features, will it be helpful?  

- *Industry Professionals (e.g., Employee at Sarvam AI)*  
1. How do you handle the hallucination of the model?  
2. What are the main difficulties you face while training the model?  
3. Which type of documents do you feed to the model?  
4. Which format have you specified for the generation of the documents?  
5. How do you maintain the privacy of the clients?  
6. Where do you see AI being used in this law-related industry in the near future?  
7. What is the token limit of the model?  
8. What type of stamp/signature do you use to authenticate the documents?  
9. Where do you gather relevant information from?  

- *Common Users*  
1. How do you resolve the confusion regarding any kind of legal queries currently?  
2. How often do you face difficulty in understanding legal documents?  
3. If you could use a simple app or website to generate a legal document, what features would you expect it to have?  
4. Would you find it useful if a system explained legal doubts/queries in easy/layman's language?  
5. How important is it for you that a real lawyer verifies the document prepared by AI?  
6. What fears or hesitations do you have in using a system that generates documents/summaries using AI?  

---

- **Summary of Interviews**  
  - *Law Students*: Struggle with complex language and formatting in legal documents, leading to confusion. They seek simplified explanations and summaries. Open to AI tools but concerned about security and reliability. Value the option to consult a lawyer alongside AI.  
  - *Common Users*: Often rely on friends, online searches, or local lawyers to resolve legal queries. Still face difficulty understanding documents. Expect a simple app that generates documents, explains terms in layman’s language, and offers lawyer verification. Main concerns: accuracy, privacy, and over-reliance on AI.  
  - *Industry Professionals*: Information gathered from government sources like High Court Ahmedabad. Formatting issues are often resolved manually but expected to improve in the future.  

---

- **Functional Requirements**  
  - Document Generation and Formatting: Allow users to generate legal documents through prompts.  
  - Document Review and Analysis: Users can upload documents and receive summaries/analysis.  
  - Connecting to Lawyers: Platform for clients to connect to lawyers when facing difficulties.  
  - E-signature and Authentication: Support digital/e-signatures and verify documents.  
  - Revenue Opportunities: Premium feature for connecting with lawyers.  
  - User Account & Document Management: Enable accounts for storing and managing legal documents.  

---

- **Non-Functional Requirements**  
  - Security/Privacy: End-to-end encryption of user data.  
  - Scalability: Handle multiple users simultaneously without crashing.  
  - Performance: Quick system responses with minimal delay.  
  - Transparency: Show differences between AI-generated and lawyer-verified documents; provide % accuracy.  
  - Accuracy: Minimize hallucinations and maintain a threshold accuracy level.  
  - Usability: Easy-to-use interface for both lawyers and users.  
  - Reliability: Use trusted LLMs and verified lawyers on the platform.  

---

- **User Stories**  

  - *Document Summary*  
    - **Front Card**:  
      As a user, I want the system to explain legal documents in easy language so that I can understand them easily.  
    - **Back Card**:  
      - System highlights difficult clauses and provides simple explanations.  
      - The system should not mislead users in case of uncertainty, and suggest lawyer assistance for such sections.  

  - *Document Generation*  
    - **Front Card**:  
      As a user, I want to generate legal documents by providing the necessary inputs so that I can use them wherever required.  
    - **Back Card**:  
      - The system should generate documents quickly.  
      - The documents must be formatted properly as per jurisdictional parameters.  

  - *Document Verification and Authentication*  
    - **Front Card**:  
      As a user, I want my generated documents to be verified and signed by experts so that I can trust their legal validity.  
    - **Back Card**:  
      - System should notify users once verification is complete.  
      - Provide proper authentication with a stamp, barcode, or digital signature.  

  - *Communication with Lawyers/Legal Experts*  
    - **Front Card**:  
      As a user, I want to communicate with lawyers/legal experts through the system so that I can get personalized help for my legal needs.  
    - **Back Card**:  
      - System should provide chat features to communicate with lawyers.  
      - Lawyer contact information shared only after consent.  

  - *Security and Privacy*  
    - **Front Card**:  
      As a user, I want my documents and personal information to remain private and secure so that I can safely use the system.  
    - **Back Card**:  
      - All user data and documents must be encrypted end-to-end.  
      - Secure authentication must be required for access.
